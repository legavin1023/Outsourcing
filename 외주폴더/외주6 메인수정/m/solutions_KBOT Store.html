Build, Test, and Share
Innovative Algorithm
We strongly believe that the existence of the KBOT Store and providing of B-Code will further facilitate competition as well as cooperation amongst bot developers. This phenomenon will eventually bring forth continuous enhancement of trading algorithms. Such goal requires learning, teaching and sharing to take place. We will provide an open-source software and a code library which allows seamless translations between multiple coding languages. Also, we will provide MOOCs for potential bot developers to materialize better ideas about algorithm and help themselves. This will benefit all participants both economically and technologically.
